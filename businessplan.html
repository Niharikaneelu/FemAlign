FemAlign Business Report

Executive Summary

Problem Statement

High stress levels, body pain, dehydration, and irregular sleep patterns significantly reduce work efficiency in the IT industry. This decline in productivity not only affects individual well-being but also impacts the overall economic growth of India.

Solution

An innovative chair helps tackle poor posture, stress, and dehydration. It adapts to different body types and stress levels using embedded sensors.

Mission

To provide AI-driven health tracking and personalized comfort solutions that revolutionize workplace wellness and enhance productivity in the IT industry.

Vision

To revolutionize workplace wellness with smart solutions that enhance productivity and quality of life.

Key Objectives
  • Improve workplace comfort
  • Enhance productivity in the IT industry
  • Encourage healthy hydration habits and overall well-being
  • Support better sleep and stress management
  • Create a scalable and marketable product
