What is Customer Profiling

Customer profiling is the process of creating detailed descriptions of an organization's ideal customers, encompassing their demographics, behaviors, needs,
and motivations. It helps businesses understand their target audience better, enabling them to tailor marketing and sales efforts to resonate with the right people and improve customer experience.
